Projects

Faculty Information System

The Faculty Information System is a web application designed to streamline the management of faculty-related information in educational institutions. It includes features such as faculty profiles, course assignments, and communication tools to enhance collaboration.

Drowsiness Detection System

The Drowsiness Detection System is an innovative project that uses computer vision techniques to detect signs of drowsiness in individuals, particularly in the context of driving. The system employs facial recognition and monitoring to alert users when signs of drowsiness are detected, promoting road safety.

AI System to Detect Plant Disease

The AI System to Detect Plant Disease is aimed at helping farmers identify and manage crop diseases. Leveraging machine learning algorithms, the system analyzes images of plant leaves to detect signs of diseases. This tool assists farmers in taking timely actions to protect their crops and improve overall yield.
